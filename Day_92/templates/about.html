The Science Behind Color Analysis

Our color extraction system uses K-means clustering, a machine learning algorithm that groups similar colors together. The algorithm analyzes every pixel in your image and identifies the most representative colors based on their frequency and similarity.

We then apply color theory principles to generate harmonious color schemes and calculate important metrics like luminance for accessibility, color temperature for mood analysis, and provide human-readable color names for better understanding.
