About The Most Dangerous Writing App

The writing app that forces you to keep writing or lose everything

How It Works

Choose Your Challenge

Select from four difficulty levels, each with different time limits:

  • Easy: 10 seconds of thinking time
  • Normal: 5 seconds of thinking time
  • Hard: 3 seconds of thinking time
  • Extreme: 1.5 seconds of thinking time
Start Writing

Once you start typing, a countdown timer begins. The timer resets every time you type a character, but if you stop typing for longer than your selected time limit, everything disappears forever.

Don't Stop!

The app monitors your typing activity continuously. Visual warnings appear when you're running out of time, with the interface changing colors to indicate danger levels.

Save Your Work

You can save your writing session at any time by clicking the Save button. Successfully completed sessions are automatically tracked with detailed statistics.

Why Use This App?

Overcome Writer's Block

The pressure to keep writing forces your brain to bypass the internal editor that often causes writer's block.

Increase Writing Speed

Regular practice with time pressure naturally improves your writing speed and fluency.

Unlock Creativity

When you can't stop to judge your words, unexpected creative ideas often emerge.

Track Progress

Monitor your writing statistics over time to see improvements in speed and endurance.

Tips for Success

Start with Easy Mode

Build confidence with the 10-second timer before attempting harder challenges.

Don't Edit While Writing

Focus on getting ideas down first. Editing can wait until after you save.

Set Small Goals

Aim for 100 words at first, then gradually increase your target.
